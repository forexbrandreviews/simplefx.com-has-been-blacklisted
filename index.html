How to verify if a broker is legitimate

1. Check regulatory licensing

Search for the broker in databases like FCA (UK), CySEC (EU), ASIC (AU), or NFA (US).

2. Compare registration details

Verify the broker’s claimed address, license number, and legal entity name against public records.

3. Use independent forums

Reddit, Forex Peace Army, Trustpilot, and other platforms can show patterns of behavior.

4. Never deposit large amounts at first

Test withdrawals with small funds before committing. Scams often show no signs until withdrawal time.
